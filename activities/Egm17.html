What is the difference between OL and UL Tags?

OL and UL are some of tags we use in computer programming languages. OL (Ordered List) is use when describe steps of a recipe, and UL (Unordered List) is use when explain aspect of category, such as, to do lists and list new vocabulary.

For example
OL (Ordered List)
  1. First
  2. Second
  3. Third

UL (Unordered List)

  • First
  • Second
  • Third
