Question : What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

The differences between both is the Block tag is starting on the new line while using it, but the inline tag is use when user wants the space out the specific content.

Question : What is the margin, border, and padding?

  • Margin - is reffer to the space outside of the border.
  • Border - is the tag uses to space the area between content and border.
  • Padding - is visible line around the content, and it depends on our need, for example, tags names dotted, thin, and thick.

Question : What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

PX(Pixel), %(percentage), ems(em), cm(centimeters), mm(millimeters). etc
